Key Features

Agilsite SEO auditor solution involves a range of features to comprehensively assess and optimize a website for search engine performance. Here are key features

On-Page SEO Analysis

Evaluates individual pages for elements such as title tags, meta descriptions, headings, image alt text, URL structure, and internal linking.

Keyword Analysis 

Helps identify relevant keywords for the site's content and assesses their competitiveness and search volume.

Content Quality and Optimization

Sample ​ Assesses content for uniqueness, relevance, readability, and offers suggestions for improvement.

Integration and Automation

Integration with other tools or platforms (Google Analytics, Google Search Console, etc.) and automation of certain tasks to streamline the SEO auditing process.

Competitor Analysis

Provides insights into competitors’ SEO strategies, keywords they rank for, and their backlink profiles.

Reporting and Insights

Generates detailed reports with actionable insights and recommendations for improvements
